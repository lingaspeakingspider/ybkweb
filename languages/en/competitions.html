COMPETITIONS

Teknofest 2023 Artificial Intelligence in Health Competition
Participation in this competition was made with the project titled "BI-RADS Category and Breast Composition Estimation in Computer Vision Screening Mammography". BioCally team, which passed the reporting stages, became a finalist among the last 19 teams among 719 teams that applied.
Participation was made in the BioGate competition held within Istanbul Technical University with the project titled "Use of Conivaptan Hydrochloride as an Inhibitor against Lactate Metabolism and Immunosuppressive System in PDAC Cancer". Competing internationally and with university teams, BioCally won first place in the "Computational Biology" category.

Tübitak 2024 Regional Competition
BioCally participated in the Tübitak 2024 competition with 3 projects: "Impairment of Lactate Metabolism in Pancreatic Ductal Adenocarcinoma Cancer by MCT1/MCT4 Inhibition", "Use of Conivaptan Hydrochloride as an Inhibitor against Lactate Metabolism and Immunosuppressive System in PDAC Cancer" and "Classification of Mammography Images with Deep Learning Approaches". 2 of them and gained the right to participate in the "Regional Competition".
In this competition, it was aimed to develop the "BI-RADS Category and Breast Composition Estimation in Computer Vision Screening Mammograms" project. New breakthroughs have been made such as mass and calcification detection with artificial intelligence on mammograms and "Entity Name Extraction from Mammography Radiology Reports".
