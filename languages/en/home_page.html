Who are we?

CALYBK is a student organization founded in 2022 with the purpose of expand biotechnological and biomedical innovations for youth. Main target of club is producing new projects like alternative treatment of cancer or cancer level prediction through artificial intelligence and endearing biology through these activities. Club structure includes 4 main departments. These are "Project Department", "PR Department", "IT Department" and "Organization Department". Club is led by an administration team. This team includes four department leader, club leader and advisor.
