Who Are We?
CALYBK is a student organization founded in 2022 with the aim of popularizing biotechnological and biomedical innovations for young people. The main goal of the club is to produce new projects such as alternative cancer treatment or estimating cancer levels through artificial intelligence and to make biology popular through such activities. The club consists of 4 main departments. These are the "Project Department", "Public Relations Department", "Information and Technology Department" and "Organization Department". The club is managed by a management team. This team consists of four sub-team leaders, a club leader and a consultant.
