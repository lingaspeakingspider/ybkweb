About Us

Our vision and goals

Research and Development
We are a club that allows students to use the school's laboratories more effectively and frequently, and encourages students interested in various branches of biology (microbiology, molecular biology, physiology, medicine, zoology, botany, ecology, biochemistry, bioinformatics, etc.) to develop projects in their fields. We guide students who develop projects in finding colleagues who are interested in the same subject, managing the project process, writing a project report, scanning scientific literature, developing hypotheses, benefiting from previous studies, etc.
Acamdemic Life
We support students who want to improve their general biology knowledge; who are preparing for exams such as TUBITAK Olympiads, AP, IB, SAT, YKS, etc. or who are working to participate in competitions, during their scientific preparations. We ensure that students develop a positive understanding not only about biology but also about the entire scientific community, and thus become people interested in science, learning and research.
Social Interaction
With social activities, we ensure that students develop teamwork, process management, group communication and leadership skills that they will need in business life. Finally, we bring students together with academics and other researchers who are experts in their fields of science so that they can benefit from their knowledge and experience.
