ABOUT US

OUR TARGET

We are a club that enables students to use the school's laboratories more effectively and frequently and encourages students who are interested in various branches of biology (microbiology, molecular biology, physiology, medicine, zoology, botany, ecology, biochemistry, bioinformatics, etc.) to develop projects in their fields. Students developing projects; finding colleagues who are interested in the same subject, managing the project process, writing a project report, scanning the scientific literature, developing hypotheses, benefiting from previous studies, etc. We provide guidance on issues. In addition, those who want to improve their general biology knowledge; TÜBİTAK Olympics, AP, IB, SAT, YKS etc. We support students who are preparing for exams or studying to participate in competitions during their scientific preparations. We ensure that students develop a positive understanding not only of biology but also of the entire scientific community, and thus become individuals interested in science, learning and research. With social activities, we enable students to develop the teamwork, process management, group communication and leadership skills they will need in business life. Finally, to bring students together with academics and other researchers who are experts in their fields of science so that they can benefit from their knowledge and experience.
